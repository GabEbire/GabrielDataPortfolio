# E-Commerce Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

The E-Commerce Sales Dashboard provides an interactive analysis of sales performance across products, countries, customers, and time. It enables stakeholders to monitor revenue trends, customer activity, product performance, and regional sales to support strategic business decisions.

---

## Business Problem

E-commerce businesses need visibility into sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance to maximize revenue and improve operational planning. This dashboard consolidates key metrics into one view, helping identify top-performing products, profitable markets, and seasonal sales patterns.

---

## Key Insights

- High-Level Performance: A total of 4,373 customers across 38 countries. It successfully processed a quantity of 5.1M units sold, generating $9.7M in total revenue.
- Exponential Growth: Yearly revenue generated showed anexponential increase between 2010 and 2011.
- Pronounced Seasonality: Revenue peaks sharply in Q4, with November marking the single highest revenue point. It drops significantly moving into Q1, with January recording the lowest performance of the year.

---

## Recommendations

- Smoothen the Q1 Revenue Drop: Run proactive, targeted marketing initiatives extending from late December straight into the new year to bridge the demand gap and avoid the typical Q1 drop-off.
- Investigate Growth Catalyst: Conduct a deep-dive operational audit to isolate the exact drivers behind the exponential revenue spike in 2011. Documenting these variables will provide the strategic blueprint needed to replicate that growth in future years (like 2012).
