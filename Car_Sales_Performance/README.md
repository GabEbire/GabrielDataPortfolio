# Car Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes analyzes car sales performance across different manufacturers, regions, body styles, and customer demographics.

---

## Business Problem

Car dealerships and manufacturers need a clear understanding of sales performance, customer buying patterns, and market trends to optimize inventory, pricing, and marketing strategies. This dashboard consolidates key sales metrics into one view, enabling faster and more informed decision-making.

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains car sales data, including:

- Car id
- Date
- Customer Name
- Gender
- Annual Income
- Dealer Name
- Company
- Model
- Engine
- Transmission
- Color
- Price ($)
- Dealer No
- Body Style
- Phone
- Dealer Region

The following cleaning steps were performed:

- Removed duplicate records

---

## Key Insights

- High-Level Performance: Across 30 different car companies, the data records a total revenue of $671.53M generated from 24K total vehicle sales.
- Seasonal & Monthly Trends: Sales experience a massive year-end surge, peaking in November (3.5K) and December (3.5K), which suggests a heavy reliance on holiday promotions and seasonal purchasing behavior.
- Customer Color Preference: Pale White is the overwhelmingly preferred color choice, accounting for roughly 47% of sales (11.3K units).
