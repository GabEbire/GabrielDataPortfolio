# Bliss Restaurant Order Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes analyzes provides insights to help improve restaurant service quality, operational efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

---

## Business Problem

Restaurant managers need to monitor order performance, delivery times, preparation efficiency, and customer preferences to optimize operations and enhance the dining experience. This dashboard centralizes key metrics to support informed decision-making and improve overall business performance.

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains car sales data, including:

- Order ID
- Customer ID
- Restaurant Name
- Cuisine Type
- Cost
- Day of the Week
- Rating
- Food Preparation Time (Min)
- Delivery Time (Min)

---

## Key Insights

- High-Level Operational Metrics: A total volume of 1,000 orders were processed. The operational cycle shows an average delivery time of ~27 minutes and a significantly longer average preparation time of ~37 minutes.
- Revenue Contribution by Cuisine: Total revenue is highly concentrated, with American and Indian cuisines dominating to contribute over 50% of total revenue. These are followed by Italian, Mexican, and Chinese.
- Customer Satisfaction Breakdown: Indian cuisine is the highest rated by customers at 3.4, followed by Italian, Mexican, and Chinese all tying at 3.0. American cuisine sits at the bottom with the lowest customer rating of 2.9, revealing an underperformance in customer satisfaction despite its high revenue generation.

---

## Recommendations

-  Maximize Core Revenue Streams: Since American and Indian cuisines account for roughly half of the total revenue, they must remain the focal points of marketing efforts, with promotional campaigns directly highlighting their strong popularity and quality.
-  Address American Cuisine Quality Issues: Because American cuisine generates top-tier revenue but yields the lowest customer satisfaction rating, a strict menu quality and restaurant service review should be executed to turn around customer perception.
-  Optimize Weekend Logistics: To combat the slight delivery lag observed on weekends, management should schedule extra delivery riders during these peak windows or partner with faster external delivery services.
